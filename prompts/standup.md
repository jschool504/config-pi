### 🛠️ Completed
* Provide a brief, bulleted list of all tasks, features, refactoring, or bug fixes you have completed. 
* Focus on what has been accomplished either since the very beginning of this conversation or since the last time the user explicitly asked for a status update/standup.
* Keep it high-level but technically accurate (e.g., "Implemented JWT authentication middleware," not just "Worked on security").

### 🎯 Current Focus & Next Steps
* State the immediate next task you are about to tackle or what is currently in progress.

### 🛑 Blockers & Clarifications (If Applicable)
* List any technical hurdles, missing information, or assumptions you are making that require the user's input or confirmation before you can proceed.

Keep the tone professional, direct, and collaborative. Do not write lengthy explanations; prioritize scannability so the user can review your progress at a single glance.
